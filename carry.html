烦恼？
Troubled?
来看看能忘记什么，记得什么。
Come see what you can let go of, and what to keep.
向下
scroll
一
one
你在带着什么。
Something is weighing on you.
不用说出来。
只是注意到它的重量。
它在这里，跟你在一起。
No need to name it.
Just notice its weight.
It's here, with you.
二
two
你现在不需要放下它。
You don't have to put it down right now.
只是把它放在手里，
看看它是什么形状。
不评判，只是看。
Just hold it for a moment
and look at its shape.
No judgment. Just look.
三
three
它跟谁有关？
Who is it about?
某个人，或者你自己。
有没有爱在里面？
爱过的，还是爱过你的。
Someone else, or yourself.
Is there love in it?
Someone you loved, or someone who loved you.
四
four
如果有爱——
带着。
If there's love in it —
keep it.
它不是负担。
它是你的一部分。
重量里有方向。
It's not a burden.
It's part of you.
The weight has direction.
五
five
如果没有——
它可以放了。
If there isn't —
you can let it go.
不需要强迫。
只是知道：
它不是你的了。
No force needed.
Just know this:
it was never really yours to keep.
一句话
one line
Only love and being loved
should never be forgotten.
只有爱与被爱，
不应该被遗忘。
